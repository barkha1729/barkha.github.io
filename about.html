Connecting the dots...

Like a Q-learning agent in a stochastic environment, my continuous learning journey has been more exploratory in the beginning than in the recent years. The rewards for me were my interest generated and impact on my immediate community. Initially, till class 12 in school, I explored my interests in various subjects including but not limited to biology, mathematics, and arts. Problem solving and mathematics were somethings that I became passionate about. Hence, pursuing engineering was the next best action. During my undergraduate studies, I explored my interests ranging from supply chain concepts to computer science concepts. Designing, programming and data mining came out to be my major interests in this exploration. I pursued them by taking related electives and competitive coding. After completing a software development internship at Reflexis, I joined as a data engineer at Core Compete. A great support from the community accelerated my small steps towards data science. In november, I proposed a NLP based idea at my workplace with the sole intent of solving an unidentified problem and, at the same time, applying my learnings. In december, I was offered a data science role. Now that I have narrowed down my field, I intend to attain depth in knowledge.
